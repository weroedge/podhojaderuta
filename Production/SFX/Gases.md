# Gas Requirements (CO2)

List of industrial gases needed for SFX and Lighting.

## 1. SFX (Special Effects)
*   **Quantity:** 16 x Large Bottles (30kg).
*   **Type:** Vapour Withdrawal.
*   **Use:** CO2 effect systems.

## 2. Lighting
*   **Quantity:** 4 x 20lb Bottles (9kg).
*   **Type:** Non-siphoned.
*   **Use:** Lighting smoke/haze machines.
