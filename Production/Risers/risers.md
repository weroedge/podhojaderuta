# Stage Risers & Rolling Decks

Requirements for band risers, drum risers, and additional stage platforms.

## Riser Specifications
- **Drum Riser:** Rolling riser with specific dimensions and carpet requirements.
- **DJ/Keys Risers:** Additional platforms needed for the band members.
- **Construction:** Must be sturdy, leveled, and fitted with locking casters and skirting.

_Refer to **Limp Bizkit 2026 Risers DRN1 AAUK.pdf** for exact dimensions, placement on stage, and height requirements for each riser._
