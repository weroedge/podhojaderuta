## 4. Important Notes and Agreements
* **Exclusions:** This logistics plan covers the main band, crew, and staff. **It does not include support acts**, who must coordinate their own transport.
* **Band and Chef:** Direct band movements will be managed by *Jerome*, and logistics for the tour chef (*Chef Pete*) will be managed directly with him.
* **Driving Regulations:** To comply with public transport regulations in Spain (vehicles over 9 seats), a mix of regular vans (no special hours restriction) and minibuses (8-hour driving limit) has been chosen.
* **Parking:** The 10 parking spots requested in the technical Rider will be used to house this fleet of runners and shuttles.
