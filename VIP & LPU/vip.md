## Operations and Logistics
- **Check-in:** LPU and VIP check-in must be physically separated (different but contiguous tables/areas). They cannot share a tent.
- **Furniture:** (4) tables and (5) chairs are required for the check-in area, installed before 10:00 AM.
- **Merchandising:**
  - **VIP Express Lane:** Exclusive lane at the exterior merch stand.
  - **Merch Prep:** Will be done inside the stadium in a secure and closed area on load-in day (06/22). No external storage container will be hired.
- **Hospitality:** Provide water dispensers and cups for Early Entry (EE) attendees while waiting in the queue area.
- **Budgets and Costs:** Detailed breakdown of security, ushers, positions and hourly costs pending (Estimated: 5 days before the show).
