📍 How to get to Auditorio Miguel Rios — Rivas-Vaciamadrid

The venue is located at Avenida de los Andes, s/n, 28529 Rivas-Vaciamadrid, Madrid.

  • By Metro: Line 9 (Paco de Lucia) + Commuter Train to Rivas-Vaciamadrid station.
  • By Commuter Train (RENFE): Line C-7 to Rivas-Vaciamadrid station, about 15 mins walk from the venue or by local taxi.
  • By car from Madrid: A-3 Highway (Valencia), exit 18 Rivas-Vaciamadrid. Follow signs to Auditorio Miguel Rios.
  • Parking: Available at the venue and surroundings. Check with the logistics department for production access.

Camera Risers Requirements

Consolidated technical information for local provision of camera structures.

1. FOH & SLASH Platforms (Long Lenses)

  • Use: Support for 2x FOH Cameras and 2x SLASH Cameras with FUINON 107x lenses.
  • Requirement: If local production does not provide the cameras, covered platforms must be provided for the tour team.
  • Stability: Critical due to the use of high magnification lenses (107x). Must be solid and vibration-free structures.
  • Protection: Include cable protection (yellow jackets/cable ramps) from the platform to connection points.

2. PTZ Camera Riser (Panasonic UE 150)

  • Location: Festival Floor or Stage Deck.
  • Requirement: Level "appropriate riser" to ensure operational height and remote control stability.
